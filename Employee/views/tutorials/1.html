Read step-by-step instructions

1. We will start by logging in using the id and password that we received from our manager.

*In addition, you can check your email account for an email sent to you by Shift Reports with your login credentials.*

2. Then, we will select the shift we are reporting for.

3.Once we successfully login, we will come to the Opening Cash Drawer.

4. The Shift reports app is composed of Opening1 and Closing2 cash drawers, Safe Drops3, MOP Sales4, Shift Details5, and the Review and Submit6 tab.

5. Let’s start with the Cash drawers. We will enter in the number of pieces of bills, coins, and rolled coins. As we enter the number of pieces of bills or coins – the app will automatically calculate the total dollar value in the drawers at the top here in green.

6. The next tab is the Safe Drops. In this tab we simply enter in the individual safe drops we made throughout our shift by entering the dollar value of each drop.

7. On the Sales Info tab, also known as the MOP sales - which stands for Method of Payments. In this tab, it is necessary to enter in the MOP Sales for at least the fields in All Registers. We have the option to enter the sales in Register 1 and 2 in addition to All Registers.

8. The Shift Details tab contains the shift times that we will enter from the POS Shift Report receipt after we closed out our shift, and a checklist that we are responsible for completing and checking off.

9. When we complete the shift and entered all the shift details from all the tabs in the app, we can review the entries in this tab. If everything looks correct, we will scroll all the way to the bottom of the page and click the "Approve and Submit Shift" button.
