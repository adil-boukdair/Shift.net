Read step-by-step instructions

1. On the opening drawer. You will enter any number of pieces of bills, coins, and rolled coins that you are counting - not the dollar value. For example, if I had 2 fifties, I would enter 2 here not 100. Your total will be displayed in green automatically.

2. If I enter 1 in the twenties field, the total will increase to 120 dollars.

3. Coins are done in the same way - if i have 3 quarters I enter in 3 not 75. As you can see the total increased by 75 cents once i entered 3.

4. Rolled coins are done the same way. You enter the number of rolled coins that you have for quarters, dimes, nickels, and pennies. For example if I have 3 rolled dimes - that would be equivalent to 15 dollars. So the total value would change to $135.75.

*You enter the information the same way for the closing drawer tab by entering the number of pieces for each type of bill, coin, and rolled coins.*
